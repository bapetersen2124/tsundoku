Tsundoku

Sit. Stay. Read.

"Even when reading is impossible, the presence of books acquired produces such an ecstasy that the buying of more books than one can read is nothing less than the soul reaching towards infinity."

 

History

"Tsundoku" originated as Japanese slang (積ん読) "tsun-doku".

「積ん読」 came from 「積んでおく」 "tsunde-oku" (to pile things up ready for later and leave) and 「読書」 "dokusho" (reading books).

Present

A.K.A. "reading pile."

Hands up if you've ever felt personally victimized by tsundoku.

Future

Despite the never-ending (and growing) pile, I'll probably keep buying books faster than I can read them.

Will you?
